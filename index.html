The Problem

Your Microsoft 365 environment is probably more exposed than you think.

Most small businesses run daily operations — email, files, client records — on Microsoft 365 without anyone actively managing its security. These gaps are rarely negligence. They're simply what happens without a dedicated specialist watching.

  • —Former employees whose accounts remain active long after departure
  • —Multi-factor authentication inconsistently enforced, or not enforced at all
  • —Administrative privileges held by more users than necessary
  • —No structured visibility into overall security posture
  • —Licensing waste from unused or misallocated seats
